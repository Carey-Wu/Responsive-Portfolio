About Me

I'm originally from South Jersey in the Medford and Cherry Hill area. I'm the youngest of 3, with an older brother and a twin brother. In my free time, I play alot of volleyball. I currently play at an A/AA level in various beach and grass tournaments, as well as indoor volleyball leagues.

I went to school at TCNJ where I graduated with a BS in Biology with a focus in Ecological studies. After college I worked for a government regulatory agency enforcing and monitoring water quality standards related to the Delaware River and its tributaries.

I currently work as a Project Manager for an industrial hygiene and safety consulting firm in their technologies division. I mainly deal with remediation and major construction projects in regards to air monitoring and related aspects. We currently use a proprietary system that we designed to monitor these kinds of projects. I'm hoping to expand my coding knowledge so that I can design the next generation system for us to use.
